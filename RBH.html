Verse of the Month — August 2025
Romans 8:31
New International Version
"What, then, shall we say in response to these things? If God is for us, who can be against us?"

This verse is part of a passage where the Apostle Paul emphasizes the security believers have in Christ. He reassures Christians that if God is on their side, no one and nothing can stand against them. It is a statement of confidence in God's protection, power, and love — encouraging believers to trust in Him no matter what challenges they face.
