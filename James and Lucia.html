Who are we?

James Loreus and Lucia Milien are two youngs who met at Church of God of Jameau. They grew in the same hood and they lived very close. Music is the pilar of their Love. They have Maried since 2012 in mission of hope of Titanyen Haiti and have got their first release in December 2012 "Chwazi Jezi". Four years later, 2016 They have got their second release " my reason why". They have made a movie in 2020 " j'ai trouve l'amour". it in Haitian creole. They are working now on a third album.
